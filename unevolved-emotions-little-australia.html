    Foreword

    While the first sentences of Little Australia were written back in 2006 it took us until 2016 to realise that our only chance to get our stories in front of our friend and families (and hopefully beyond this inner circle) was to start by finishing our stories with a the style of short stories. This is the first edition is hence the logical outcome of this enlightenment.

    While this is actual fact, as well as theories mentioned in this book, it should be noted that all characters are fictional and any similarities with existing or previous individual would be total fortuity. The author, Konemara
